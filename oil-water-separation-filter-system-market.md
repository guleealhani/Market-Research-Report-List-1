<p><p>The oil-water separation filter system market is categorized into three main types: mechanical-based, chemical-based, and membrane-based systems. Mechanical-based systems utilize physical methods such as gravity separation or skimmers to remove oil from water. Chemical-based systems employ flocculants or coagulants to enhance separation through chemical reactions. Membrane-based systems utilize porous membranes to selectively filter out oil and contaminants from water. Each type offers distinct benefits, catering to diverse industrial applications and wastewater treatment needs.</p></p>
