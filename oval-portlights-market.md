<p><strong>The Oval Portlights Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Opening</li><li>Standard</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oval portlights are specialized marine fixtures designed for efficient ventilation and light within enclosed spaces, such as boats and yachts. The market comprises two main types: opening and standard. Opening portlights provide the functionality of being able to pivot or slide open, allowing for adjustable airflow and ease of access. Standard portlights, on the other hand, are fixed units that do not open but offer reliable sealing and protection against water ingress, making them ideal for areas needing consistent light without ventilation.</p></p>
