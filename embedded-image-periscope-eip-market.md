The market is primarily divided into two types: penetrating and non-penetrating. The penetrating type involves installations that extend through surfaces, providing direct access to the desired viewpoint, while the non-penetrating type remains external, relying on external components for functionality. Both types cater to diverse applications in fields like medical imaging, industrial inspection, and surveillance, addressing different user needs and installation contexts.</p></p>
