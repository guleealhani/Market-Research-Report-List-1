<p><ul><li>Claus Process</li><li>Claus Process and Tail Gas Treatment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sulfur recovery market primarily comprises three main types: the Claus process, which efficiently converts hydrogen sulfide into elemental sulfur, is widely used for its low operational costs and high recovery rates; the modified Claus process, which enhances the traditional method's efficiency, often incorporating additional reactors for improved sulfur recovery; and tail gas treatment, focusing on the further recovery of sulfur compounds from the Claus process's exhaust, ensuring environmental compliance and maximizing sulfur yield. Together, these processes optimize sulfur recovery and minimize emissions.</p></p>
