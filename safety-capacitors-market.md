<p><strong>The Safety Capacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class-X Capacitors</li><li>Class-Y Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The safety capacitors market is primarily divided into Class-X and Class-Y capacitors, each serving unique functions in electrical systems. Class-X capacitors are designed for use across mains power lines, preventing high-frequency noise and offering voltage capabilities. Class-Y capacitors, on the other hand, provide safety against electrical shock by connecting between the mains and ground, ensuring that leakage currents are safely shunted. Both types are essential for compliance with safety standards in electronic devices and appliances, enhancing performance and reliability.</p></p>
