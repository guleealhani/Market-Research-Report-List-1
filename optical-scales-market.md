<p><strong>The Optical Scales Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Optical Scales</li><li>Absolute Optical Scales</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical scales market is primarily divided into two types: incremental optical scales and absolute optical scales. Incremental optical scales provide position feedback relative to a reference point, enabling precise measurements over short distances. They are typically used in applications requiring real-time position tracking. In contrast, absolute optical scales offer a unique position reference for each measurement, eliminating the need for recalibration after power loss. This makes them ideal for applications demanding high accuracy and reliability in various industrial settings.</p></p>
