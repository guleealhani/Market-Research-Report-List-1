<p><p>The Bio Pond Filter market comprises three key types of filtration: physical, chemical, and biological. Physical filtration involves the removal of debris and particles from pond water using screens or mesh. Chemical filtration utilizes substances like activated carbon to eliminate toxins and impurities, enhancing water clarity and quality. Biological filtration fosters beneficial bacteria that convert harmful ammonia and nitrates into less harmful substances, promoting a balanced ecosystem. Together, these filtration methods ensure a healthy and sustainable aquatic environment.</p></p>
