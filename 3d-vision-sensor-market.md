<p><strong>The 3D Vision Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stereo Vision</li><li>Structured Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D vision sensor market is primarily categorized into stereo vision and structured light technologies. Stereo vision uses two or more cameras to capture images from different angles, enabling depth perception and object detection. In contrast, structured light projects a known pattern onto a scene, allowing for precise 3D measurement based on the deformation of the pattern. Both technologies are widely utilized in robotics, automation, and augmented reality applications, enhancing machine understanding of physical environments and facilitating interaction.</p></p>
