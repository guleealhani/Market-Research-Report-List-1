<p><p>License Plate Recognition (LPR) cameras come in two main types: mobile and fixed. Mobile LPR cameras are typically mounted on vehicles, allowing for flexible deployment and the ability to capture license plates while in motion, making them ideal for law enforcement and parking enforcement activities. Fixed LPR cameras, on the other hand, are stationary units installed at specific locations such as toll booths, parking facilities, or city entry points. These provide consistent monitoring and data collection for security and traffic management.</p></p>
