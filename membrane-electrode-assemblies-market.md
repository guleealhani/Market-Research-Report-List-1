<p><p>The Membrane Electrode Assemblies (MEAs) market is primarily categorized into three types: 3-layer MEA, 5-layer MEA, and other configurations. In contrast, the 5-layer MEA includes additional catalyst and gas diffusion layers, enhancing performance and efficiency, particularly in fuel cells.
