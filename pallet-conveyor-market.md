<p><strong>The Pallet Conveyor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drag Chain</li><li>Roller (Driven or Gravity)</li><li>Plastic Type</li><li>Slat Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pallet conveyor market consists of various types, each designed for specific applications. Drag chain conveyors utilize chains to move heavy pallets efficiently, while roller conveyors can be either driven or gravity-fed, allowing for flexible material handling. Plastic type conveyors are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and ideal for food and pharmaceutical industries. Slat type conveyors consist of interlocking slats, providing a sturdy surface for heavy items, often used in automotive manufacturing and assembly lines, enhancing productivity and reliability in material handling.</p></p>
