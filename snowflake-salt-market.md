<p><ul><li>Granule</li><li>Lamellate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Snowflake salt is categorized into two primary market types: granule and lamellate. Granule snowflake salt consists of larger, coarse crystals, ideal for culinary uses and seasoning due to its crunchy texture. On the other hand, lamellate snowflake salt features thin, delicate flakes, which dissolve quickly and are often used as a finishing salt, enhancing dishes with a subtle burst of flavor. Both types cater to different culinary applications, appealing to various consumer preferences in food preparation and presentation.</p></p>
