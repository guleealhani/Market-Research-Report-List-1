<p><p>The automatic milking machines market is categorized into semi-automatic and fully-automatic systems. Semi-automatic milking machines require some manual intervention, allowing farmers to control aspects of the milking process while benefiting from mechanization. In contrast, fully-automatic milking machines operate independently, automatically milking cows without human involvement, enhancing efficiency and productivity. Both types cater to different farming needs, with semi-automatic systems being suitable for smaller operations and fully-automatic systems ideal for larger dairy farms seeking maximum automation and output.</p></p>
