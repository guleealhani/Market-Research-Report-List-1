<p><p>AI-based fever detection cameras are categorized by their operating wavelength: short-wave, mid-wave, and long-wave length cameras. Short-wave length cameras capture images in visible light and are useful for initial visual inspections. Mid-wave length cameras detect infrared radiation, providing more accurate temperature readings from a distance, enhancing fever detection accuracy. Long-wave length cameras focus on thermal imaging, effectively identifying body heat and identifying individuals with elevated temperatures. Each type serves distinct applications in health monitoring and security settings.</p></p>
