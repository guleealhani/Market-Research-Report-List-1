<p><ul><li>Low Pressure System</li><li>Medium Pressure System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV pool systems utilize ultraviolet light to disinfect and purify pool water, offering two main market types: low-pressure and medium-pressure systems. Low-pressure systems operate at lower intensities and are primarily used for residential pools and smaller commercial applications, providing effective disinfection with energy efficiency. In contrast, medium-pressure systems operate at higher intensities, enabling greater water flow rates and rapid pathogen destruction, making them suitable for larger commercial pools or water treatment facilities where higher disinfection demands exist.</p></p>
