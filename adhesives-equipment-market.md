<p><p>The adhesives equipment market includes various types designed for different bonding needs. Cold glue equipment utilizes water-based adhesives that cure at room temperature, ideal for delicate materials. Hot melt equipment uses thermoplastic adhesives applied in a molten state, providing quick bonding for various substrates. Guns and applicators are tools that facilitate the application of adhesives, enhancing precision and efficiency. Additionally, accessories such as nozzles, cleaning tools, and adhesive containers support the effective use and maintenance of adhesive equipment.</p></p>
