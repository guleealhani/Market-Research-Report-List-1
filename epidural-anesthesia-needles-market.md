<p><p>The epidural anesthesia needles market includes specialized types like Huber point needles and wave point needles. Huber point needles feature a unique angled tip that facilitates access to the epidural space while minimizing tissue damage, making them ideal for catheter placements. Wave point needles, on the other hand, utilize a curved design that enhances precision during insertion and reduces the risk of complications. Both needle types are crucial for effective pain management in various medical procedures, contributing to their growing market demand.</p></p>
