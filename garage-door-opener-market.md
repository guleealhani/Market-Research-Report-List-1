<p><strong>The Garage Door Opener Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Belt Drive Openers</li><li>Chain Drive Openers</li><li>Screw Drive Openers</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The garage door opener market is primarily categorized into three types: belt drive, chain drive, and screw drive openers. Belt drive openers use a rubber belt for quiet and smooth operation, making them ideal for residential settings. Chain drive openers utilize a metal chain for durability and strength, suitable for heavy doors. Screw drive openers operate using a threaded steel rod, providing fewer moving parts and requiring less maintenance. Additionally, "Others" encompasses innovative technologies like smart openers and direct drive systems.</p></p>
