<p><p>Hypromellose Acetate Succinate (HPMCAS) is categorized into three grades based on viscosity and solubility: L Grade, M Grade, and H Grade. L Grade offers low viscosity and is ideal for immediate-release formulations. M Grade has medium viscosity, suitable for a range of applications including controlled-release products. H Grade, with high viscosity, is often used in sustained-release formulations. These grades cater to diverse pharmaceutical needs, influencing drug delivery systems and enhancing bioavailability in various dosage forms.</p></p>
