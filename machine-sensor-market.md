<p><p>The machine sensor market includes various types designed to measure specific physical parameters. Force and torque sensors gauge mechanical loads and rotational forces. Temperature sensors monitor thermal conditions, while pressure sensors detect fluid or gas pressures. Position sensors track the location of objects. Proximity sensors identify the presence of nearby objects without direct contact. Collectively, these sensors enhance automation, improve safety, and optimize operational efficiency across diverse industries, including manufacturing, automotive, aerospace, and healthcare.</p></p>
