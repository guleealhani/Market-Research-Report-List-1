<p><p>Rockwool roof insulation comes in three primary market types: blanket, board, and others. Blanket insulation consists of flexible rolls that provide easy coverage for irregular spaces and contours, making it ideal for large areas. Board insulation features rigid panels, offering high compressive strength and effective thermal performance for flat or sloped roofs. The "others" category includes products like batts, loose-fill, and specialty solutions tailored for specific applications. Each type enhances energy efficiency, noise reduction, and fire resistance in building structures.</p></p>
