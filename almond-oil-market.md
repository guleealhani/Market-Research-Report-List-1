<p><p>The almond oil market is primarily divided into two types: sweet almond oil and bitter almond oil. Sweet almond oil, extracted from the edible seeds, is widely used in cosmetics, cooking, and health products due to its nourishing properties. In contrast, bitter almond oil, derived from the bitter variety of almonds, is primarily used in aromatherapy and for industrial purposes, as it contains amygdalin, which can be toxic if not processed correctly. Both types cater to distinct consumer needs and applications.</p></p>
