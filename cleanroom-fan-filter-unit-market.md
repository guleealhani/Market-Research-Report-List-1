<p><p>The Cleanroom Fan Filter Unit (FFU) market features two main types: those equipped with HEPA (High-Efficiency Particulate Air) filters and ULPA (Ultra-Low Penetration Air) filters. HEPA filter units effectively capture 99.97% of particles down to 0.3 microns, making them suitable for controlled environments requiring high air cleanliness. In contrast, ULPA filter units provide even greater filtration, capturing 99.999% of particles, including smaller sizes, ideal for industries like pharmaceuticals and biotechnology that demand stringent contamination control.</p></p>
