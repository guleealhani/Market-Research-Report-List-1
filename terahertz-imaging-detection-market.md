<p><p>The terahertz imaging detection market is divided into two main types: passive and active terahertz imaging. Passive terahertz imaging captures naturally emitted radiation from objects, allowing for non-invasive visualization without any external energy source, commonly used in security and surveillance applications. In contrast, active terahertz imaging involves generating terahertz waves through an external source, facilitating improved image quality and detection capabilities in various fields, including biological imaging and material characterization. Both methods offer unique advantages tailored to specific applications.</p></p>
