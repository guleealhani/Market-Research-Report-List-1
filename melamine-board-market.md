<p><strong>The Melamine Board Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Melamine Faced Chipboard</li><li>Medium Density Fiberboard</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The melamine board market includes primarily two types: melamine faced chipboard (MFC) and medium density fiberboard (MDF). MFC consists of particleboard coated with melamine resin, providing durability and aesthetic appeal for furniture and interior applications. In contrast, MDF consists of wood fibers bonded by resin, offering a smooth surface for intricate designs and easy machining. Both materials are popular in the furniture industry, emphasizing cost-effectiveness and versatility, catering to diverse consumer preferences in home and commercial spaces.</p></p>
