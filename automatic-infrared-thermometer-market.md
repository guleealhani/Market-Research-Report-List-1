<p><p>The automatic infrared thermometer market is categorized into three main types: portable, online, and scanning types. Portable infrared thermometers are handheld devices ideal for quick temperature checks in various settings. Online types are installed in fixed locations, typically used for continuous temperature monitoring in environments like food processing or industrial applications. Scanning types are designed for rapid temperature screening of multiple individuals, commonly used in health settings to efficiently identify fevers in crowded environments, enhancing public health safety.</p></p>
