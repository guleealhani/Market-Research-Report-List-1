<p><p>The seat elevator market comprises two main types: curved rail seat elevators and straight rail seat elevators. Curved rail seat elevators are designed to navigate around corners and curves in staircases, making them ideal for homes with complex layouts. In contrast, straight rail seat elevators are installed along straight staircases, providing a simpler and more cost-effective solution for those with straightforward stair designs. Both types enhance accessibility for individuals with mobility challenges, offering a safe and convenient means of navigating stairs.</p></p>
