<p><ul><li>Full Container Load (FCL)</li><li>Less-than Container Load (LCL)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Land Freight Forwarding Market is divided into Full Container Load (FCL) and Less-than Container Load (LCL) segments. FCL involves shipping an entire container dedicated to one shipper, optimizing space and providing cost efficiency for large shipments. In contrast, LCL combines multiple small shipments from different shippers into a single container, allowing for cost-effective solutions for businesses with smaller volumes. Both types cater to diverse logistical needs, balancing efficiency and budget considerations in freight transport.</p></p>
