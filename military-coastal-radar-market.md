X-band radars operate at shorter wavelengths, providing high-resolution imagery ideal for detecting smaller targets and detailed surveillance in coastal environments. S-band radars, on the other hand, cover larger areas with better long-range capabilities, making them suitable for tracking larger vessels and weather monitoring. Both radar types are crucial for enhancing coastal security, surveillance, and maritime situational awareness.</p></p>
