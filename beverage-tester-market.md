<p><p>The beverage tester market is primarily divided into two types: handheld beverage testers and benchtop beverage testers. Handheld beverage testers are portable instruments designed for quick field measurements, ideal for on-site quality control and immediate analysis. In contrast, benchtop beverage testers are larger, stationary devices typically used in laboratories for comprehensive testing and analysis of beverage qualities such as pH, turbidity, and alcohol content. Both types serve crucial roles in ensuring beverage quality and safety in the industry.</p></p>
