<p><p>The Medium Density Polyethylene (MDPE) resins market is characterized by two primary production processes: the autoclave and tubular methods. The autoclave process involves polymerizing ethylene under high pressure in a large reactor, producing MDPE with specific properties for applications like packaging and pipes. The tubular process, on the other hand, uses a continuous flow system to create resin, which typically results in higher output and improved molecular weight distribution. Both methods cater to diverse industrial needs across various sectors.</p></p>
