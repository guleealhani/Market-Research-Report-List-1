<p><ul><li>Open Isolator</li><li>Closed Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aseptic isolators market is categorized into two main types: open and closed isolators. Open isolators allow for direct exposure to the environment, typically used in applications involving non-hazardous materials, while maintaining product sterility during processes like compounding. Closed isolators, on the other hand, provide a fully contained environment, offering greater protection against contamination and often used for handling hazardous materials or sensitive biological products. Both types play crucial roles in pharmaceutical, biotech, and laboratory settings to ensure sterility and safety.</p></p>
