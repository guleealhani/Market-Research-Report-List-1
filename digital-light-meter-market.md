<p><ul><li>Reflected-Light</li><li>Incident-Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital light meter market is primarily divided into two types: reflected-light and incident-light meters. Reflected-light meters measure the light that bounces off a surface, making them suitable for evaluating specific areas or subjects, such as artwork or scenes in photography. Conversely, incident-light meters measure the light falling onto a subject, providing a more accurate representation of the lighting conditions affecting the scene. Both types cater to professionals in photography, cinematography, and various industrial applications for optimal lighting control.</p></p>
