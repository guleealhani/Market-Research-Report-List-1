<p><strong>The Broadband Data Card Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>GSM</li><li>CDMA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The broadband data card market primarily consists of two types: GSM and CDMA. GSM (Global System for Mobile Communications) cards use a SIM-based architecture, allowing users to access data services via cellular networks. They are widely adopted due to their compatibility with various devices and international roaming capabilities. Conversely, CDMA (Code Division Multiple Access) cards utilize a different technology that doesn't require a SIM, focusing on voice and data transmissions over radio frequencies. Both types cater to diverse user needs in mobile internet access.</p></p>
