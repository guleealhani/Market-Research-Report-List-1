<p><p>The palletizer market consists of two main types: traditional palletizers and robotic palletizers. Traditional palletizers utilize mechanical systems to stack products onto pallets, often requiring more space and extensive maintenance. In contrast, robotic palletizers employ automated robotic arms that offer flexibility, adaptability, and higher efficiency, allowing for the handling of various product types with minimal changeover time. Both types serve the logistics and manufacturing sectors, catering to increasing demands for automation and efficiency in supply chain processes.</p></p>
