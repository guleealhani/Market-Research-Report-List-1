<p><strong>The Automatic Calorimeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Differential scanning calorimeter</li><li>Oxygen bomb calorimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic calorimeter market comprises various types, notably the differential scanning calorimeter (DSC) and the oxygen bomb calorimeter. DSC measures heat flow changes in materials as they are heated or cooled, providing insights into thermal transitions. Conversely, the oxygen bomb calorimeter determines the heat of combustion by combusting a sample in a controlled environment, crucial for evaluating fuel values. Both types serve specific applications in research, materials science, and industries concerned with energy content and thermal properties.</p></p>
