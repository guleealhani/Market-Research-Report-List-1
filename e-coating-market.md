<p><strong>The E-coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cathodic</li><li>Anodic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The E-coating market is primarily divided into two types: cathodic and anodic. Cathodic E-coating involves the use of negatively charged particles that are deposited onto a positively charged substrate, providing superior corrosion resistance and durability. Anodic E-coating, conversely, uses positively charged particles on a negatively charged surface, offering good color retention and a smooth finish. Each type serves different applications and industries, catering to specific performance and aesthetic requirements in coatings for various metal products.</p></p>
