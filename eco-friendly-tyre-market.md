<p><p>The eco-friendly tyre market features two primary types: all-steel and semi-steel tyres. All-steel tyres use a steel structure throughout, offering enhanced durability, lower rolling resistance, and increased fuel efficiency, making them ideal for heavy-duty vehicles. In contrast, semi-steel tyres combine steel belts with a fabric body, providing a balance between flexibility and strength, suitable for passenger vehicles. Both types prioritize sustainable materials and manufacturing processes, contributing to reduced environmental impact while maintaining performance and safety standards.</p></p>
