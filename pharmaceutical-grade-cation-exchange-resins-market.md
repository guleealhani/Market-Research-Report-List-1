<p><p>The pharmaceutical grade cation exchange resins market is divided into strong acidic and weakly acidic types. Strong acid cation exchange resins are used for their high capacity to exchange cations at varying pH levels, making them ideal for purifying and deionizing water in pharmaceutical applications. Weakly acidic cation exchange resins, on the other hand, are suitable for selectively removing specific cations and are often used in delicate processes. Each type serves distinct roles in ensuring product purity and efficiency in pharmaceutical manufacturing.</p></p>
