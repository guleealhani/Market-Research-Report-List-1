<p><p>The wound dressing pad market is categorized primarily into non-stick pads and sticky pads. Non-stick pads are designed to prevent adherence to the wound, minimizing pain during removal and promoting healing by allowing for easy drainage. In contrast, sticky pads have an adhesive backing that securely attaches to the skin, providing a protective barrier against contaminants while allowing for easy application. Both types cater to different wound management needs, ensuring optimal care and recovery based on wound characteristics.</p></p>
