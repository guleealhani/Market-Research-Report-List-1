<p><strong>The Temporary Bonding Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Slide－off Debonding</li><li>Mechanical Debonding</li><li>Laser Debonding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Temporary Bonding Adhesive market encompasses three primary debonding types: Thermal Slide-off Debonding utilizes heat to weaken the adhesive bond, allowing easy removal. Mechanical Debonding involves physical methods, such as peeling or pulling apart surfaces, to detach components. Laser Debonding employs focused light to selectively break down the adhesive at targeted points, facilitating precise separation without damaging underlying materials. Each method offers unique advantages for various applications, making them suitable for industries like electronics and automotive.</p></p>
