<p><p>The large hydro turbine runner market comprises several key types, including Francis, Kaplan, and Pelton turbines. Francis turbines are versatile, ideal for medium to high head applications, utilizing a spiral casing and adjustable blades for efficient flow. Kaplan turbines, suited for low head sites, feature adjustable blades that optimize performance in varying water flows. Pelton turbines excel in high head conditions, employing a unique bucket design to harness kinetic energy from free-flowing water. Other types may include emerging technologies and variations.</p></p>
