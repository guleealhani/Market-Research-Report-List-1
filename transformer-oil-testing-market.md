<p><strong>The Transformer Oil Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dissolved Gas Analysis</li><li>Moisture Analysis</li><li>Dielectric Breakdown Voltage</li><li>Flash Point</li><li>Interfacial Tension</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transformer oil testing market includes several critical analysis types to ensure the integrity and performance of electrical transformers. Dissolved Gas Analysis (DGA) identifies gaseous byproducts from oil decomposition, indicating potential failures. Moisture Analysis assesses water content, crucial for dielectric strength. Dielectric Breakdown Voltage testing evaluates the oil's insulating properties. Flash Point measures the oil's flammability risk, while Interfacial Tension evaluates oil purity and potential contaminants. Other tests may include viscosity and presence of particulates, enhancing overall transformer reliability.</p></p>
