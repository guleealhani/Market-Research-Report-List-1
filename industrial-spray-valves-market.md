<p><ul><li>Linear Spray Valves</li><li>Radial Spray Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial spray valves market comprises two main types: linear spray valves and radial spray valves. Linear spray valves deliver a directed, consistent spray pattern ideal for applications requiring precision, such as in coating or cleaning processes. Conversely, radial spray valves distribute fluid in a circular pattern, making them suitable for broader coverage applications like cooling or surface treatment. Each type is designed to optimize fluid delivery, enhancing efficiency and effectiveness in various industrial applications.</p></p>
