<p><p>The iron and steel cutting fluid and oil market consists of various types, including water-based cutting fluids, cutting oils, and emulsions. Water-based cutting fluids are typically dilutable with water, offering cooling and lubrication while being environmentally friendly. Cutting oils are petroleum-based, providing superior lubrication and heat resistance but may have environmental concerns. Emulsions combine oil and water, offering a balance of cooling and lubrication, making them versatile for various machining processes. Each type serves specific operational needs in metalworking.</p></p>
