<p><strong>The Mobile Cardiac Telemetry Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lead Based Devices</li><li>Patch Based Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mobile Cardiac Telemetry Monitors market is primarily segmented into lead-based devices and patch-based devices. Lead-based devices utilize electrodes attached to the skin, providing continuous monitoring of heart activity through traditional wires, ensuring high fidelity in data collection. Conversely, patch-based devices offer a more discreet and convenient solution, using adhesive patches that can be easily applied to the skin for wireless monitoring. Both types enhance patient mobility while delivering real-time cardiac data to healthcare providers for timely intervention.</p></p>
