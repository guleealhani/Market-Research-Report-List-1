<p><p>The telecom power supply market comprises three main types: linear, switched, and battery-based systems. Linear power supplies provide stable output but are often bulky and less efficient. Switched power supplies, on the other hand, are compact and efficient, utilizing high-frequency switching to convert electrical power. Battery-based systems offer backup power for telecom operations, ensuring reliability during outages. Each type serves specific needs, balancing factors such as efficiency, size, and backup capability to support the telecom infrastructure effectively.</p></p>
