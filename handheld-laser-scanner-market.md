<p><p>The handheld laser scanner market encompasses various interfaces that facilitate data transmission between the scanner and connected devices. The SCSI interface is known for high-speed data transfer, suitable for industrial applications. EPP (Enhanced Parallel Port) provides improved data rates, commonly found in specialized systems. USB (Universal Serial Bus) offers versatile connectivity and ease of use, being the most widely adopted. PS2 interfaces, although less common now, were previously used for connecting scanners to computers, particularly in legacy systems. Each interface caters to specific user needs and operational environments.</p></p>
