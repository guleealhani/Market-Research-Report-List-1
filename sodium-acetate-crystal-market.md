<p><p>The Sodium Acetate Crystal market comprises two primary types: Sodium Acetate Anhydrous and Sodium Acetate Trihydrate. Sodium Acetate Anhydrous is a dry form, commonly used in chemical processes, food preservation, and as a buffering agent. In contrast, Sodium Acetate Trihydrate contains water molecules and is often utilized in heat packs and laboratory applications due to its ability to release heat upon crystallization. Both types play vital roles in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food, and textiles.</p></p>
