<p><p>The medical adhesives and sealants market comprises three main types: acrylic, silicone, and rubber. Acrylic adhesives offer strong bonding properties and quick-setting capabilities, making them suitable for various applications in medical devices. Silicone adhesives are valued for their flexibility, biocompatibility, and durability, often used in wound care and prosthetics. Rubber adhesives provide excellent elasticity and adhesion, ideal for orthotics and prosthetic devices. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring effective medical applications while prioritizing patient safety and comfort.</p></p>
